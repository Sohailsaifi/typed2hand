What are we about?

One day, Emma was very tensed about the assignments she has to submit in her college, because She fell on the stairs and had a wrist sprain. She was unable to hold a pen.

On talking about this issue to her friend Tom, he suggested Typed2hand as a solution to her problem.

Are you facing the some similar problem as Emma did?

So, let's hear what Tom says about Typed2hand.


Typed2hand converts your typed Documents into handwritten ones.

Step 1 : Pick a Page


Here, either you can choose one from the wide variety of pages available or you can upload your own institution's Text.


Step 2 : Pick a Font


You can adjust the combination of font-style, font-color and font-size as the one which resembles your handwriting.


Step 3 : Type it


Use your fast typing skill to type the Documents in the editor and get it in the handwritten form.


Step 4 : Download it


You can now download/export it in the .png, .jpg, .jpeg or .pdf format and get it printed in order to submit in the college.


Now, as Typed2hand saves a lot of your time, you can now spent your valuable time in learning some practical stuffs.
